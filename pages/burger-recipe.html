    Burger Sauce Ingredients

    These are the simple ingredients you'll need to make this restaurant-worth burger sauce recipe:

  • Condiments:A flavorful mixture of ketchup,mayonnaise,and mustard is the base.
  • Seasonings:This burger sause is seasoned with dried minced onion, dried minced garlic, and seasoned pepper.
  • Hot sauce:Hot sauce (to tase) spices things up.
  • Vinegar:A dash of vinegar adds a bit zing and balances the other ingredients.

    ingredients:

  • 1 cup mayonnaise
  • ½ cup ketchup
  • ¼ cup prepared yellow mustard
  • 2 teaspoons dried minced onion
  • ¼ teaspoon dried minced
  • ⅛ teaspoon white vinegar
  • hot sauce
  • seasoned pepper to taste
